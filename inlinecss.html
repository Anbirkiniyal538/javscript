BUTTERFLY

Butterflies are the adult flying stage of certain insects belonging to an order or group called Lepidoptera.
Moths also belong to this group.
The word "Lepidoptera" means "scaly wings" in Greek.
Butterflies are insects that have large, often brightly coloured wings, and a conspicuous, fluttering flight.
The group comprises the superfamilies Hedyloidea and Papilionoidea.
The oldest butterfly fossils have been dated to the Paleocene, about 56 million years ago, though they may have originated earlier.
